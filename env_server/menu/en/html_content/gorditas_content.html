What’s with the ‘N?

You’ll spot little ‘N tags on names like Gorditas’n and Frijolitos’n.
That’s how rancheros in Jalisco say it when they’re talking fast, warm, and full of flavor.
It’s not grammar, it’s seasoning. Just a little extra spice at the end of the word.

Así se les dice a esta sabrosísima
preparación de bolitas de masa
rellenitas de guisados
tradicionales.
